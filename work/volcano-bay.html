Constraints

The TapuTapu‚Ñ¢ band relies on an ecosystem of technology to be effective, including queuing points for each ride (ride totems), way-finding screens (village totems), and totems for guests willing to pay a premium (cabana totems).

Leaving No One Stranded

Ride Totems

Kiosks shaped like Polynesian totem poles that allow users to access the digital line for each ride. Every ride has it‚Äôs own set of ride totems.

Private Cabanas

Guests in groups can reserve cabanas for a day, which come equipped with a special totem and a few extra perks. These totems allow users to access digital lines and food and beverage sales all from the comfort of the shade.

Village Totems

Village totems are static screens placed throughout the park that display wait times, a map, and advertisements.
