Fueling Discovery

Interactive discovery components, taxonomy systems and a redefinied architecture help users truly explore over 2,00 pages worth of science content. Explore a selection of components we designed below:

Science Shuffle

The Science Shuffle component appears throughout the site. It allows users to see related content, browse by the topics they are interested in - or roll the dice and discover something completetly new.

Social Shuffle

Users can also explore through their own lens. Using an integration with CrowdRiff, we built a component that uses Instagram galleries of real Field Museum guests to generate calls to action that highlight points of interest on the site.
